What you need to Begin

  1. a Wetsuit
  2. a Leash
  3. a Surfboard
  4. Surf Wax to keep the top of your board sticky
  5. lastly Sunscreen

Step 1: How to pick out a board

Type of Board Attributes Difficulty
Soft Top longboard very floaty and easy to stand up on. Beginner
Soft Top shortboard Has float, but is more maneuverable. Beginner/intermediate
Fiber glass longboard Paddle well but is less floaty Intermediate
Fiber glass shortboard Not floaty, but is really good for doing turns and carves. Advanced
